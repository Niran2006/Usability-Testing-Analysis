Key Findings

Positive Findings

  • Easy onboarding experience
  • Simple habit completion process
  • Clean visual design
  • Fast navigation

Pain Points

  • Analytics lacked clarity
  • Settings difficult to locate
  • Small reminder icon
  • Limited onboarding guidance

Design Improvement Suggestions

Improve Analytics

Add chart labels, legends, and explanatory tooltips.

Enhance Navigation

Move settings into bottom navigation.

Increase Visibility

Use larger reminder and notification icons.

Better Onboarding

Add guided walkthrough for first-time users.

Conclusion

The usability study revealed that users successfully completed most tasks and appreciated the application's simplicity. However, analytics interpretation and settings discoverability require improvements. Based on collected feedback and observed behavior, several design refinements were proposed to enhance overall user satisfaction and task efficiency.
